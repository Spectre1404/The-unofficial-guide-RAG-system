UNIVERSITY RENTALS
Apartments for UIC Students — Little Italy, University Village & Rush Medical District

For over two decades, University Rentals has specialized in residential properties surrounding the University of Illinois Chicago campus. We manage and lease apartments favored by UIC undergraduates, graduate students, medical residents, and Rush University Medical Center affiliates who want walkable or short-commute housing in established Chicago neighborhoods.

Why UIC Students Choose University Rentals

- Portfolio concentrated near East Campus, West Campus, and Taylor Street
- Familiarity with academic-year leasing cycles and May turnover
- On-site maintenance coordinators for most properties
- Online tenant portal for rent payment and maintenance requests

We are not affiliated with UIC. We participate in the UIC Off-Campus Housing Listing Service and welcome student applications with co-signers when required.

Neighborhoods We Serve

Little Italy / Taylor Street
Heart of UIC student social life. Properties range from renovated vintage walk-ups to mid-rise units two to eight blocks from Lecture Center. Evening dining and CTA bus lines on Taylor and Halsted.

University Village / Maxwell Street
Newer construction and condo-rental units near Roosevelt CTA (Blue, Green, Orange, Pink lines). Popular with students who want in-unit laundry and elevator buildings.

Rush Medical District / Polk Corridor
Studios and two-bedrooms serving nursing, pharmacy, public health, and medical students with rotations at Rush, UI Health, and nearby clinics.

Halsted / Harrison Corridor
The practical "between campuses" zone — minimizes daily travel for students with classes on both East and West campus.

Application Requirements

- Completed application per adult occupant
- Application fee: $65 per applicant
- Credit and background check
- Proof of enrollment or employment
- Income requirement: 2.5–3x monthly rent combined household income
- Co-signer accepted for students without sufficient credit history
- Security deposit: typically one month's rent per Chicago custom

Lease Terms

Standard 12-month lease, August or September start preferred for student groups. Some studios available for 6-month or individual lease start dates. Subletting requires written approval. Renter's insurance required at move-in.

Proximity to Campus

Properties are located 0.3 to 1.2 miles from UIC East Campus, 0.2 to 0.8 miles from key West Campus facilities. U-Pass covers unlimited CTA use. Many residents walk year-round; winter gear matters.

Maintenance and Tenant Support

Tenants submit non-emergency maintenance through the online portal. Emergency issues (no heat in winter, major water leak, lockout) call the 24-hour line posted in each lease. Response targets: urgent within 24 hours, routine within 3–5 business days. Document all requests in writing.

Move-in and move-out inspections use a standardized checklist. Tenants photograph existing damage at move-in to protect security deposits. Chicago RLTO governs deposit return timelines; itemized deductions must be provided when applicable.

Sublease Policy

Students planning study abroad or summer away may request sublease approval. Submit proposed subtenant application for landlord review. Unauthorized subletting violates most leases. UIC sublease board is a common place to find qualified subtenants familiar with academic schedules.
